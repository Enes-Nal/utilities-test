general questions

How Much Does Our Program Cost?

Our program is entirely free. We made this program accessible because we believe everyone has the right to learn these topics.

How Can I Sign Up?

If you go to our home page, there is a sign-up button, which will take you to the form. It will ask you your basic information alongside the classes you want to take.

Why should I join this program?

You should join this program to enrich your school with some classes that might not be offered by the school you are attending. Also, in the end, you will be awarded a certificate. The level of that certificate will depend on the effort you put into this program.

Will this program continue over the summer?

Our program ends in May, yet we will have a program during the summer.

Are we going to get a certificate at the end of this program?

Yes. Students will be awarded a certitiface that corresponds to the effort they put in this program.

Do you need any background knowledge to participate in the program? Can we start at the beginners level?

You do not need to know anything. You can start from any knowledge level at any time! Our instructors will do their best to give you the best possible experience.

Where can we ask our questions about the program?

If you go to our Contact page, you will see a button that reads "Click Here to Contact Us" If you click it, you will be taken to a form where you can submit any of your questions to us.

How can this program help me with my college applications?

This program can help you get experience in topics that you are interested in studying as well as be something you can put in your resume.

Which grade levels are accepted into this program?

To participate in this program, you have to be a high school student. But depending on the maturity, we can accept 8th graders.
