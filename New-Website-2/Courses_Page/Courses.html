Course Language(s) Course Description
Algebra 1/2 English/Turkish This will be an Algebra course in which you will learn to reason symbolically. The key content involves writing, solving, and graphing linear and quadratic equations. Our classes will be held on zoom and the amount I will be able to teach will depend on the speed of the class. You will be able to ask your questions through zoom chat or by unmuting yourself during class. We will use email to communicate with each other outside of class.
Korean English
History English The students are given a knowledge and understanding of the world’s history. We will take a look at and examine the people, and events that shaped our modern world. The students will critically think about history. We will not only learn about certain events, but also why they happened and what kind of effects it had, and still has to this day.
Making Beats/Music English/Turkish In this class I will be talking about the history of music and it’s founders. Also help the students engage by creating beats/music with what they have. There will be assignments of creating simple one minute beats and research to answer some questions.
Body Building English/Turkish In Bodybuilding class, I am going to teach you about workout. I will go into details like what you need to do to workout, what you need to eat to stay healthy and more. We will go over 1 topic every week and will give little homeworks about the topic I taught you. I will give you some goals to achieve about Bodybuilding.
ESL(English Second Language) English This class is a continuation of basic communication in students' new language, with further listening and speaking development. The formal skills of writing, reading, and vocabulary, along the lines of everyday situations, interesting contexts, and a variety of material.
Japanese Japanese Hello, in this class I will teach beginner level japanese. I would like to go over these brief topics. Pronunciation, speaking skills, and reading and writing. The goal I would like to achieve is for everyone to have a good foundation with the language. Whether it be good at reading or writing, or being able to hold a conversation in japanese.
Grammar English An overview of grammar rules that many students get confused with. Each basic grammar rule will be gone over which includes punctuation, misspelling, subject-verb agreement, sentence fragments, etc. Students will test their understanding by asking questions and doing quizzes.
Flute English/Turkish In this course we will start learning the basics of the flute. It will include how to hold it, making sounds, and learning simple melodies on it. This will be everyone's starting point to an improvement.
