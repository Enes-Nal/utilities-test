Welcome to Borderless Tutors

Borderless Tutors are providing free tutoring for students from anywhere in the world

What is this program?

Borderless Tutors provides free tutoring for students anywhere in the world. We give our students a selection of classes that vary from algebra 1&2 to body building. You can see all the classes we offer in our courses section with a course description for each class.
Signing up is as easy as clicking the "Apply Now" button and filling out the registiration form it takes you to. You will be asked some personal info such as your name, you grade level, your parents or legal guardians name etc.
Then, you will get to pick what classes you would like to take. You can pick multiple classes, but you would have to list those classes from the class you are most interested in to the class you would be okay with not taking. You will then be requested to explain why you are interested in taking that specific course.

Why Borderless Tutors?

Completely Free


We do not charge our students since we believe education should not be obtained in exchange of any type of currency

Variety of Classes


We offer over 10 classes from conversation practice to body building to Korean. Students get to choose which ones they would like to take from the registiration form.

Online


Our classes are online, which is easier for our students to learn since they are learning at the comfort of their own homes.




Who Are We?

Borderless Tutors has been created, and is being ran by high school students across America. Every single one of our members have a strong passion of helping, not specifically those in need, but helping in a way that could potentially open many doors for people in the future.
That is exactly what our program does. We teach our students skills and different languages they may not ever have a chance to learn ever again in their life.
At the end of our program, we award our students with a certificate depending on the students performance in classes. On top of learning new skills, our students will receive an award that will help with their college applications.



Our Curriculum

On top of being completely free, our program offers a wide variety of classes from ESL to flute. Students can choose up to 3 classes while filling out our application form.

Students will then be interviewed by our instructors as the final part of the enrollment process. All of the classes we offer are located at our curriculum page alongside their course descriptions provided to you in a chart.
